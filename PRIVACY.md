# Privacy Policy — Animal Spin

**Effective date:** June 30, 2026
**Developer:** Circuit Stitch
**Contact:** kyle@circuitstitch.com

Animal Spin is an offline app for young children. We designed it to collect
**nothing**.

## What we collect

Nothing. Animal Spin does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information from anyone, including children.

## How that's enforced

- The app has **no internet permission** — it is technically incapable of
  sending any data off your device.
- There are **no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs**.
- Text-to-speech uses your device's built-in **on-device** voices; nothing you
  hear is sent to any server.
- The only data the app stores is your voice/pitch/speed **settings**, kept
  locally on the device. It never leaves the device and is removed when you
  uninstall the app.

## Children's privacy (COPPA, GDPR / GDPR-K)

Animal Spin is directed to children. Because the app collects no personal
information and has no internet access, it does not knowingly collect data from
children — or from anyone — in any jurisdiction, and complies with the U.S.
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data
Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this same
location with a new effective date.
